Warranty Coverage

EDC BELTS – EDC belts, including the Low Profile and Hybrid, have a lifetime warranty that covers craftsmanship and defects.

CUSTOM WRAPPED BELTS – Custom wrapped belts have a one-year warranty that covers craftsmanship.

BATTLE AND DUTY BELTS (1.75″ AND 2″) – Battle and Duty Belt systems, including both inner and outer belts, have a one-year warranty that covers craftsmanship and defects.

ALL NON-BELT PRODUCTS – All other Blue Alpha products have a one-year warranty that covers craftsmanship and defects.

NON-BLUE ALPHA PRODUCTS – Products made by other manufacturers are covered under their respective warranty policies. Visit the manufacturer's website for details — links can be found on the product listing on our website.
